FAQs (the stuff that stops people clicking)

Will I definitely get the £50?

Only if the referral offer is active, you are eligible, and the switch completes under the current terms. You will see the offer details during sign-up.

Will my energy supply stop during the switch?

No. Switching is admin only, your supply stays on.

Do I need to tell my current supplier?

Usually no. Your new supplier handles the switch process for you.

How long does switching take?

It depends on the type of switch and supplier timings. Many switches complete in days, but it varies.

Can I use a comparison site and still get the referral?

Often no. If the £50 matters, start from the referral link, not from a comparison journey.

What if I forget to use the link?

Some suppliers can add it after sign-up, some cannot. If you forget, contact support as soon as possible and ask.
